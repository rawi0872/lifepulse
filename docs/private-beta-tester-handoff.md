# Life Pulse Private Beta Tester Handoff

## Private Beta Purpose

Life Pulse is in private beta. The goal is to test real daily use, clarity, reliability, and comfort on phone, tablet, and desktop.

This is not a public launch yet. We are looking for honest feedback from trusted testers before making broader promises.

## Tester Setup Steps

1. Open the production beta URL: https://lifepulse-sand.vercel.app
2. Create an account.
3. Confirm your email if the app asks you to.
4. Complete onboarding.
5. Try it on your phone and desktop if possible.
6. Optional: add Life Pulse to your home screen or install it as a PWA if your browser offers that option.

## First 10-Minute Path

For the first pass, please try this short path:

1. Finish onboarding.
2. Open Today.
3. Add one task.
4. Add one habit.
5. Write one short journal reflection.
6. Add one goal or project if you want to test planning.
7. Check Weekly Review, Insights, and Coach.
8. Open Settings and look at the available modules.
9. Open Devices and note that it is preview only.

## What To Test

Please focus on whether the app feels clear, useful, reliable, and comfortable in normal daily use.

Good areas to test:

- Signup and login clarity.
- Onboarding clarity.
- Whether Today feels useful as a morning command center.
- Tasks, habits, goals, and projects flow.
- Journal and Knowledge clarity.
- Weekly Review, Insights, and Coach usefulness.
- Body, Mind, Finance, and Passions clarity.
- Phone and tablet layout.
- PWA install or home-screen behavior.
- Empty states when you have not added data yet.
- Confusing wording.
- Bugs, errors, broken pages, or slow loading.

## What Not To Expect Yet

Life Pulse is early. Please do not expect these yet:

- No native iOS or Android app yet.
- No watch app yet.
- No wearable or device sync yet.
- No push notifications yet.
- No offline sync yet.
- No AI automation or external AI processing yet.
- No bank connections.
- No medical, mental health, financial, legal, tax, or investment advice.

## Safety And Privacy Notes

- Use test-safe personal data if you prefer.
- Do not enter highly sensitive information during beta.
- Finance is for manual tracking and reflection only. It is not financial, tax, investment, or legal advice.
- Body and Mind are wellness and productivity tracking areas only. They are not medical or mental-health advice.
- Coach is rule-based and transparency-focused. It is not an AI therapist, doctor, financial advisor, lawyer, or professional advisor.
- Devices are preview only. There are no automatic device or wearable data imports.

## Best Feedback To Collect

The most helpful feedback is specific and honest. Please tell us:

- What confused you?
- What felt useful?
- What felt unnecessary?
- Where did you get stuck?
- What did you expect to happen?
- What phone, tablet, or desktop did you use?
- Did anything overflow, overlap, or look broken?
- Did you trust the wording?
- Would you use Today every morning?
- What would make you come back tomorrow?

Screenshots or short screen recordings are very helpful if something looks broken or confusing.

## Known Limitations

- Life Pulse is in private beta.
- Some modules are early and may change.
- Offline mode is not supported yet.
- There is no native mobile app yet.
- There is no watch app or wearable/device sync yet.
- The data model is still evolving.
- UI polish is still ongoing.
